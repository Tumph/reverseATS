    Special Job Requirements: Students are responsible for ensuring they are eligible for an 8-month work term before applying. Please discuss your work/study sequence flexibility with your academic advisor as soon as possible.
    Job Summary: We are seeking an internship student to assist with pipeline integrity engineering, focusing on pipeline fitness-for-service (FFS) evaluation, pipeline integrity management, compliance, and automated reporting tools. This internship provides hands-on experience in developing engineering algorithms, database tools, and automation systems relevant to the oil & gas industry. The successful candidate will leverage AI-based tools to enhance pipeline integrity evaluation, optimize data processing workflows, ensure regulatory compliance, and develop predictive maintenance solutions using advanced computational techniques.
    Job Responsibilities: The intern will be responsible for developing FFS evaluation algorithms by implementing models based on CSA Z662 and API 579 standards. They will utilize AI-driven tools to automate defect detection and classification within pipeline inspection datasets, develop and validate machine learning algorithms to assess pipeline conditions, predict failure rates, and estimate remaining service life. Deep learning models will be integrated for image analysis of pipeline scans, enabling faster and more accurate defect identification.
    The intern will also be involved in developing a pipeline integrity management database by designing and implementing a structured database to manage pipeline integrity data efficiently. They will develop AI-powered tools for data collection, comparison, and evaluation, improving the accuracy and reliability of pipeline assessments. Predictive analytics will be implemented for proactive pipeline maintenance, utilizing historical data and real-time monitoring systems to anticipate failure points. Additionally, various AI techniques will be used to extract meaningful insights from engineering reports and regulatory documents.

    Another key responsibility is the development of automated reporting and documentation tools. The intern will implement AI-based automated reporting systems using natural language generation to create detailed maintenance and compliance reports. Intelligent data visualization tools will be developed to transform complex datasets into interactive and actionable insights. The intern will automate anomaly detection and generate real-time alerts using AI-driven monitoring frameworks. AI-powered chatbots or digital assistants will be created to streamline communication between engineers and integrity management teams. Additionally, the intern will work on ensuring that pipeline integrity solutions adhere to regulatory compliance requirements and industry best practices.
    Required Skills: Familiarity with engineering standards (for example, CSA Z662, API 579, ASME codes, etc.) and their integration with AI-driven analysis tools is preferred. Experience in database management, big data analytics, and cloud-based computing is highly desirable. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to develop innovative solutions for real-world engineering challenges, are crucial.

    Candidates should have a strong understanding of CAD, engineering principles, signal processing and image analysis, with experience in AI-based defect detection methods. Proficiency in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn is essential. Advanced knowledge of Python (or other relevant programming languages) is required for developing AI-powered solutions.
    Transportation and Housing:
    One-way airfare to Calgary will be provided at a reasonable price. The student is responsible for securing their own housing accommodations.
    Compensation and Benefits:
    The pay range for this internship is minimum wage up to $20/hour, depending on qualifications. The intern will have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in AI-driven pipeline integrity management solutions, network with industry professionals and AI experts, and gain exposure to cutting-edge technologies such as machine learning, computer vision, and automation in the energy sector. There is potential for future full-time employment based on performance and contributions to the project.
