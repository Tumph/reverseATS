    The Full-Cycle Service job board contains pre-approved co-op opportunities for undergraduate and graduate co-op students.

    Next steps:
    • Review how to search for jobs on WaterlooWorks using various techniques (e.g. quick search, keywords, clusters, advanced search and filters/sorts). You can also reference WaterlooWorks Help: Search for additional information.
      • Tip: Use the filter by cluster function to search for jobs that employers have identified as being a good fit for the type of work you're interested in, or various academic programs that align with your skills.
      • Tip: Try using the keyword "remote" to search for opportunities that could be completed from any location.
    • Curious about where other co-op students have worked in the past? Check out our Where co-op students work dashboard!
    • Learn how to apply for jobs on WaterlooWorks.
    Note: Though job postings are reviewed by Co-operative Education to ensure they are legitimate and suitable for co-op credit, it can sometimes be difficult to catch postings that contain fraudulent information. Please exercise caution and sound judgment when responding to any job posting, even after you have accepted a job. If you have questions or concerns about a job on the Hire Waterloo Co-op job board, contact your co-op advisor. Learn more about how you can protect yourself from job posting scams.
