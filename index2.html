Co-op Jobs

Full-Cycle Service

Use this job board (formerly the Hire Waterloo Co-op board) to connect with thousands of employers across industries looking for Waterloo talent. Benefits include: 
 
  • Jobs are pre-approved for co-op credit. 
  • Jobs align with Waterloo's work term sequences. 
  • Jobs are supported by our hiring cycles in WaterlooWorks (e.g., interview, rank/match). 
  • Consider multiple opportunities at the same time before making decisions/submitting rankings. 

  View jobs
 

Employer-Student Direct

Use this job board to apply for opportunities outside of our hiring cycles. Benefits include: 
   
  • Jobs are pre-approved for co-op credit. 
  • Apply, interview and accept offers directly on an employer-driven timeline. 
  • Apply to jobs that are continuously posted throughout the term. 
  • Secure a co-op position before the recruiting term for your scheduled work term. 
  • Simulate a post-graduation job search. 

If opportunities on this job board do not align with your work term sequence, you'll need a sequence change. If you receive a direct offer from an employer, submit an employer-student direct form for co-op credit through WaterlooWorks. 
 
View jobs
 
Please note students are encouraged to consider opportunities on all jobs boards.
